◆ support_config_pin

inherit (vsf_peripheral_capability_t) uint8_t is_async uint8_t vsf_gpio_capability_t::support_config_pin

If not supported, then vsf_gpio_config_pin() cannot be used

Asynchronous GPIO they are only guaranteed to be sequential when operating the current port consecutively.

When they are used together with other ports, they are not guaranteed to be sequential for the operation of the pins of different ports.

uint8_t vsf_gpio_capability_t::pin_count

available pin mask may be: 0x000000FF (8 pins), 0x0000FFFF (16 pins), 0xFFFFFFFF (32 pins), 0xFFFFFFFE (32 pins, but pin0 cannot be used as GPIO)
