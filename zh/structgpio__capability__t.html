◆ is_support_config_pin

inherit (vsf_peripheral_capability_t) uint8_t is_async uint8_t gpio_capability_t::is_support_config_pin

Asynchronous GPIO they are only guaranteed to be sequential when operating the current port consecutively.

When they are used together with other ports, they are not guaranteed to be sequential for the operation of the pins of different ports.

uint8_t gpio_capability_t::pin_count

total number of pins of hardware, even if some pins cannot be configured as GPIO. if some of the pins cannot be used as GPIO,the corresponding bit in avail_pin_mask is 0, otherwise is 1.

◆ avail_pin_mask

uint32_t gpio_capability_t::avail_pin_mask

available pin mask may be: 0x000000FF (8 pins), 0x0000FFFF (16 pins), 0xFFFFFFFF (32 pins), 0xFFFFFFFE (32 pins, but pin0 cannot be used as GPIO)
